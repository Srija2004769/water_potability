Understanding Water Quality Parameters

Water quality assessment is crucial for ensuring safe drinking water. This prediction system analyzes nine key water quality parameters to determine if water is safe for human consumption. Each parameter has specific acceptable ranges defined by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). Below are detailed descriptions of each parameter and their safe ranges.
